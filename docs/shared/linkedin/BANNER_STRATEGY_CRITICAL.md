## THE 5 SLOTS (CONTENT STRATEGY)

### Slot 1: The Thesis (Convergence)
*   **Concept:** Establish the unique value proposition immediately. You are an Industrial Designer operating as a Head of Engineering.
*   **Text Layer:**
    *   **Headline:** Industrial Design Mindset applied to Frontend Architecture.
    *   **Sub-headline:** From Artifacts to Systems. From Manufacturing to Engineering.
    *   **Visual Directive:** High-contrast split. Left: Physical Mold (Injection). Right: Component Tree (React). Connected by a "Mental Model" line.

### Slot 2: Design for Manufacturing (The Economic Reality)
*   **Concept:** Cost control and viability. DFM (Design for Manufacturing) translates to Performance Engineering.
*   **Text Layer:**
    *   **Headline:** Design for Engineering (DFE).
    *   **Core Message:** "A UI design is useless if it bankrupts the rendering budget."
    *   **Metric/Insight:** Managing technical constraints as creative boundaries. Viability = Strategy.
    *   **Visual Directive:** Blueprint aesthetic. Showing "Tolerances" in code vs. "Tolerances" in physical parts.

### Slot 3: Cognitive Ergonomics (The Human Factor)
*   **Concept:** DX (Developer Experience) is not about comfort, it's about reducing error rates and "injury" (burnout/debt).
*   **Text Layer:**
    *   **Headline:** Cognitive Ergonomics & Developer Experience.
    *   **Core Message:** The "User" of the Architecture is the Developer.
    *   **Key Insight:** Reducing Cognitive Load = Increasing Velocity & Quality.
    *   **Visual Directive:** Ergonomic analysis diagram (anthropometry) overlaid on a clean code structure / API surface.

### Slot 4: Supply Chain Management (The Risk)
*   **Concept:** BOM (Bill of Materials) = `package.json`. Managing dependencies is managing risk.
*   **Text Layer:**
    *   **Headline:** Supply Chain Management (Digital BOM).
    *   **Core Message:** `package.json` is a liability, not just a list.
    *   **Key Insight:** Auditing the digital supply chain for security, sustainability, and long-term maintenance.
    *   **Visual Directive:** Exploded view (Despiece) of a product where parts are labeled as dependencies (NPM packages).

### Slot 5: The Synthesis (Strategic Leadership)
*   **Concept:** The outcome of this convergence. Resilience and Adaptability.
*   **Text Layer:**
    *   **Headline:** Systemic Leadership.
    *   **Core Message:** Orchestrating Technology, Empathy, and Business Goals.
    *   **Closing Statement:** Building resilient socio-technical systems, not just apps.
    *   **Visual Directive:** A synthesis symbol. Minimalist. Dark background. "Jesús Mejía | Strategic Lead".
